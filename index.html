SlauTeams is an organisation at the Unversity of St.Lawrence that was started by Kintu Ha to help serve his university better and help it achieve its vision That is To be the leading ICT and enterpreneurship institution of higher learning in Learning, teaching, research and community services in Africa and beyond. . The organisation has different departments which are reffered to as teams. Each team is responsible for executing a specific task at the university.

SlauTeams has helped the university to produce well trained graduates that have deeper knowledge of what they have studied. This has been possible by allowing students server other students through their selected teams. Some students have went on and started their businesses from scratch while still at campus and as soon as they graduate, they tend to focus on their businesses and with help still from the SlauTeams, some businesses that were started by the members of SlauTeams have been able to blossom into successful ventures. Thanks to Kintu Ha for his brilliant idea.
